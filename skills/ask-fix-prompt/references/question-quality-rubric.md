# Question Quality Rubric

Score each dimension from 1 (poor) to 5 (strong).

## 1. Depth
Does the question force a non-superficial answer?

## 2. Answerability
Can a strong model answer it without unbounded speculation?

## 3. Predictability of answer shape
Is the expected answer structure obvious and bounded?

## 4. Evidence burden
Is the question demanding enough to reduce fluff without requiring impossible evidence?

## 5. Neutrality
Does the question avoid smuggling in a preferred conclusion?

## 6. Decision relevance
Would the answer change understanding, action, or prioritization?

## Hard fail conditions
Any of the following should fail the pack:
- conclusion baked into the question,
- topic still too broad,
- answer could be generic essay filler,
- output structure unspecified,
- disagreement handling omitted when central to the topic.
